# Known Limitations

- MIC breakpoints and ECOFFs may be drug- and species-specific.
- Binary resistant/susceptible conversion should not be done without a
  prespecified rule.
- Markers transferred from other Mollicutes are hypotheses, not validated rules.
- Absence of a marker is not proof of susceptibility.
- Literature extraction may be incomplete until source records, full text, and
  supplementary files are reviewed.
- Benchmark results will depend on the quality, representativeness, and
  comparability of the curated isolate-level data.
