# Evidence Grading

This preliminary grading system is for organizing evidence strength during
curation. It does not by itself validate a marker as a clinical decision rule.

## Level 1

Clinical genotype-phenotype association measured in the same isolate set.

## Level 2

Experimental or in vitro selected support, including laboratory selection,
engineered variants, transformation experiments, or other direct experimental
evidence.

## Level 3

Inferred or transferred rule from a related species. These entries must be
clearly labeled as transferred hypotheses unless validated in the target
species and drug context.

## Level 4

Candidate marker without sufficient validation. These entries may be useful for
tracking hypotheses but should not be treated as benchmark-ready rules.
